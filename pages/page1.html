The Pentagram

You are a lamb, the last of your kind, all used as sacrifice to the three old gods. You've just woken, after a whole life of running, captured. You are surrounded by hooded creatures holding spears. Other weapons sit at their hips, daring you to fight back. They border both sides of a dark path. You are forced to follow the trail they line, poked and prodded in the direction of a sort of stage, where three hulking figures stand. You are pushed into the center of a sticky pentagram, and the figures lean over you. After a booming speech to their followers, they order one forward. A quick movement, and you are slaughtered.
