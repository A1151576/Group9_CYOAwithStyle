The Cult Maker

With the aid of your new master, you rise again, back in the blood-splattered pentagram. You defeat the followers of the three gods that slaughtered you, and they flee, but you will face them again someday. You devote your new life to the one bound in chains. You find others who have been taken captive by the cult that once held you, or are otherwise endangered in the harsh wilderness of this world, and save them in order to gain new followers. As your following grows, so does your power.
